🌱 Aeroponics System

What is Aeroponics?

Aeroponics is a method of growing plants in an air or mist environment without soil. Roots are suspended in the air and misted with nutrient-rich water.

Key Benefits

  • Uses 95% less water than traditional farming
  • Faster plant growth rates
  • No soil needed
  • Better nutrient absorption
  • Space efficient

Required Components

  • Water pump and misting nozzles
  • Nutrient solution
  • Grow chamber or container
  • Light source (LED)
  • Control system (timer)
